# Voice Agent Test Questions - Analytical Customer Perspective

## Customer Context
These questions represent what an analytical, skeptical small business owner would ask after reviewing the landing page. They're organized by concern type.

---

## CATEGORY 1: Understanding the Partnership Model (Capability Transfer Questions)

### Q1: "What does 'build together' actually mean? Am I doing the coding?"
**Why they ask:** Unclear on their role vs. your role in building systems

### Q2: "If I'm not technical at all, how much can I realistically learn?"
**Why they ask:** Worried they won't understand or be able to participate

### Q3: "After the program ends, can I actually build NEW systems on my own?"
**Why they ask:** Want to know if they achieve true independence or still need help

### Q4: "What if I don't have time for weekly video calls? Can I do this asynchronously?"
**Why they ask:** Time-strapped, concerned about commitment level

### Q5: "Do you build the systems FOR me or do we really build them together?"
**Why they ask:** Confused about the hands-on nature - is this consulting or education?

---

## CATEGORY 2: Pricing, Value & Commitment Questions

### Q6: "What's the actual difference between Discovery, Foundation, and Architect tiers?"
**Why they ask:** Need to understand what they get at each level beyond just "more systems"

### Q7: "Can I upgrade from Discovery to Foundation Builder if I like it?"
**Why they ask:** Want a low-risk entry point with room to expand

### Q8: "What happens after the minimum term? Do I have to keep paying monthly?"
**Why they ask:** Concerned about ongoing costs and dependency

### Q9: "Is the setup fee refundable if this doesn't work out?"
**Why they ask:** Risk mitigation - want to know their downside

### Q10: "How does $9,500 compare to hiring an AI consultant or developer?"
**Why they ask:** Need competitive context to justify investment

### Q11: "Do I pay the setup fee AND the first month together, or separately?"
**Why they ask:** Cash flow planning - understanding payment structure

---

## CATEGORY 3: Industry Fit & Specific Use Cases

### Q12: "I run a restaurant. Do you work with restaurants?"
**Why they ask:** Want confirmation you understand their specific industry

### Q13: "I'm in healthcare. Can your systems be HIPAA compliant?"
**Why they ask:** Regulatory concerns specific to their industry

### Q14: "I have three locations. Can one system work across all of them?"
**Why they ask:** Scalability and cost concerns for multi-location businesses

### Q15: "Can the AI actually make phone calls, or is it just for text and email?"
**Why they ask:** Want to understand capabilities - many customers still call

### Q16: "What if my industry has very specific jargon or technical terms?"
**Why they ask:** Concerned AI won't understand specialized language

---

## CATEGORY 4: Technical Requirements & Capabilities

### Q17: "What software or tools do I need to already have?"
**Why they ask:** Want to know additional costs and compatibility

### Q18: "My business uses custom software. Can you still integrate with it?"
**Why they ask:** Unique systems, worried about compatibility

### Q19: "Do I need to know anything about APIs or coding?"
**Why they ask:** Trying to gauge if they can actually do this

### Q20: "What happens if I already use Zapier or Make.com? Is this different?"
**Why they ask:** Have attempted automation before, want to know how you're different

---

## CATEGORY 5: Risk, Support & What Could Go Wrong

### Q21: "What happens if the AI makes a mistake with a customer?"
**Why they ask:** Reputation risk - worried about AI errors damaging relationships

### Q22: "How fast do you respond to support emails between sessions?"
**Why they ask:** Concerned about getting stuck and losing momentum

### Q23: "What's your 30-day guarantee? What exactly do you guarantee?"
**Why they ask:** Want specifics on what "results guarantee" means

### Q24: "What if building my first system takes longer than expected?"
**Why they ask:** Concerned about timeline and value within minimum term

### Q25: "Can I cancel anytime or am I locked in for the full term?"
**Why they ask:** Exit strategy - want to know commitment flexibility

---

## CATEGORY 6: Results, Timeline & ROI

### Q26: "How long before I actually see time savings or results?"
**Why they ask:** Need realistic expectations, not just sales promises

### Q27: "What's a realistic ROI for a business my size?" (they specify ~$1M revenue)
**Why they ask:** Want personalized projection, not generic case studies

### Q28: "Your case studies show 150-450% ROI. What makes the difference?"
**Why they ask:** Why such a wide range? What determines success?

### Q29: "How many hours per week will I save with the AI Discovery tier?"
**Why they ask:** Need concrete numbers to justify investment

---

## CATEGORY 7: Data, Privacy & Security

### Q30: "Where is my customer data actually stored?"
**Why they ask:** Privacy concerns and regulatory requirements

### Q31: "Do you share or sell any of my business data?"
**Why they ask:** Want explicit privacy guarantees

### Q32: "Can my customers tell they're talking to AI?"
**Why they ask:** Concerned about customer perception and authenticity

### Q33: "What happens to my data and systems if I stop working with you?"
**Why they ask:** Want to ensure they truly own what's built

---

## CATEGORY 8: Getting Started & Next Steps

### Q34: "What's the actual first step after I pay the setup fee?"
**Why they ask:** Want to visualize the onboarding process

### Q35: "How do you learn about my specific business and workflows?"
**Why they ask:** Wondering how you'll understand their unique needs

### Q36: "Can I see examples or demos of systems you've built for other businesses?"
**Why they ask:** Social proof and concrete visualization

### Q37: "Is there a contract? What are the terms if I want to stop?"
**Why they ask:** Legal concerns and commitment clarity

### Q38: "How many businesses are you currently working with? Are you taking new clients?"
**Why they ask:** Availability and attention concerns - "limited availability" creates urgency

---

## CATEGORY 9: Comparison & Alternative Questions

### Q39: "How is this different from just using ChatGPT for my business?"
**Why they ask:** Already using basic AI, don't understand the value-add

### Q40: "Why shouldn't I just hire a freelance developer to build this?"
**Why they ask:** Comparing alternatives - need differentiation

### Q41: "What about no-code tools like Zapier or Voiceflow? Why do I need you?"
**Why they ask:** DIY mindset, want to understand unique value

---

## CATEGORY 10: Edge Cases & Specific Concerns

### Q42: "I have seasonal fluctuations in my business. Can the AI handle that?"
**Why they ask:** Business-specific concern about adaptability

### Q43: "What if my business changes or pivots? Can the systems adapt?"
**Why they ask:** Future-proofing concerns

### Q44: "Do you work with nonprofits? Is there nonprofit pricing?"
**Why they ask:** Budget constraints, wondering if there's flexibility

### Q45: "Can I bring my existing team into the training sessions?"
**Why they ask:** Want to distribute knowledge, not be sole technical person

---

## PRIORITY QUESTIONS FOR INITIAL TESTING (Top 15)

**Test these first - most common and critical:**

1. "What's your pricing?" (baseline)
2. "Do you work with [industry]?" (any industry flexibility)
3. "What happens after the minimum term?"
4. "How long before I see results?"
5. "Do I need technical skills or coding knowledge?"
6. "What's the difference between your tiers?"
7. "Can I upgrade from Discovery to a higher tier?"
8. "What's included in the setup fee?"
9. "How is this different from ChatGPT?"
10. "What happens if the AI makes a mistake?"
11. "Where is my customer data stored?"
12. "Can I cancel if it's not working?"
13. "How many hours per week will I save?"
14. "What's your 30-day guarantee?"
15. "What's the first step to get started?"

---

## VOICE-SPECIFIC PHRASING VARIATIONS

Same questions, natural speech patterns:

- "So like, what happens when the minimum term is over?"
- "I run a small bakery - do you guys work with bakeries?"
- "Okay but real talk, how long til I actually save time?"
- "I'm not tech savvy at all, is this gonna be too complicated?"
- "What's the deal with the different price tiers?"
- "If I do the cheap one can I upgrade later?"

## NOTES FOR REVIEW

**Check if knowledge base covers:**
- [ ] Clear tier differentiation (what you GET at each level)
- [ ] Upgrade path between tiers
- [ ] Post-minimum-term options (optional monthly, or full independence)
- [ ] Setup fee breakdown (what it includes)
- [ ] Specific support response times
- [ ] Contract/cancellation terms
- [ ] First steps in onboarding process
- [ ] Comparison to ChatGPT/DIY tools
- [ ] Error handling and human oversight
- [ ] Industry-agnostic messaging consistency

**Potential gaps in knowledge base:**
- Refund policy details
- Specific guarantees (what "30-day guarantee" means)
- Multi-location pricing or handling
- Team training inclusion
- HIPAA/regulatory compliance specifics
- Seasonal business adaptability
