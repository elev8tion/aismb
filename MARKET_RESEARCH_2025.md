## 1. AI Adoption is SURGING Among SMBs

### Current Adoption Rates (2025)

- **58%+** using generative AI (up from 40% in 2024)
- **25%** have already integrated AI into daily operations
- **50%+** actively exploring AI implementation

**Source:** [Thryv Survey 2025](https://investor.thryv.com/news/news-details/2025/AI-Adoption-Among-Small-Businesses-Surges-41-in-2025-According-to-New-Survey-from-Thryv/default.aspx), [QuickBooks Survey](https://colorwhistle.com/artificial-intelligence-statistics-for-small-business/)

### The Gap is Closing
- **Feb 2024:** Large businesses used AI at 1.8x the rate of small businesses (11.1% vs 6.3%)
- **Aug 2025:** Gap shrunk dramatically - SMBs at 8.8%, large business at 10.5%

**Source:** [SBA Research Spotlight](https://advocacy.sba.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Research-Spotlight-AI-in-Business-Small-Firms-Closing-In_-092425.pdf)

### Adoption by Size
