## Case Study 1: Local HVAC Company
**Industry:** Home Services
**Annual Revenue:** ~$1.2M
**Employees:** 8 (owner, 5 techs, 2 admin)
**Tier:** Foundation Builder ($9,500 total)

### The Challenge
- Owner spending 15 hours/week on scheduling, follow-ups, and customer communication
- Admin staff manually entering job data into QuickBooks
- Missing follow-up opportunities for maintenance contracts
- Inconsistent customer experience during busy season

### Systems Built Together
1. **Customer Communication Agent** (Week 1-4)
   - Handles appointment scheduling via text/email
   - Sends automated follow-ups and reminders
   - Answers common customer questions 24/7

2. **Job Data Automation System** (Week 5-8)
   - Automatically extracts job info from tech notes
   - Populates QuickBooks with job costs and time
   - Flags opportunities for maintenance contracts

3. **Maintenance Contract Agent** (Week 9-12)
   - Identifies customers due for seasonal maintenance
   - Sends personalized offers based on equipment age
   - Schedules appointments automatically

### Results (After 3 Months)
- **Owner time saved:** 12 hours/week (from 15 to 3 hours on admin tasks)
- **Admin efficiency:** 8 hours/week saved on data entry
- **Revenue impact:** 23% increase in maintenance contract sign-ups
- **Customer satisfaction:** Response time improved from 4 hours to 15 minutes

### ROI Calculation
- **Time savings value:** 20 hours/week × $75/hour × 12 weeks = **$18,000**
- **New maintenance revenue:** 15 new contracts × $400/year = **$6,000 annual**
- **Total value in 3 months:** $24,000
- **Investment:** $9,500
- **ROI:** 153% in 3 months

### Owner Quote
*"I learned how to build these systems myself. Now I'm thinking about what else we can automate. This changed how I think about running my business."*

## Case Study 2: Boutique Marketing Agency
**Industry:** Professional Services
**Annual Revenue:** ~$850K
**Employees:** 5 (2 partners, 3 staff)
**Tier:** AI Discovery ($4,000 total)

### The Challenge
- Spending 10+ hours/week on proposal writing for similar projects
- Client reporting manually compiled from multiple platforms
- Lead qualification inconsistent across team
- No systematic way to track project profitability

### System Built Together
**Intelligent Proposal Generator** (Week 1-8)
- Analyzes past winning proposals
- Generates customized proposals from client intake form
- Pulls relevant case studies and pricing automatically
- Maintains brand voice and compliance requirements

### Results (After 2 Months)
- **Proposal time:** Reduced from 4 hours to 45 minutes per proposal
- **Proposals sent:** Increased from 3/week to 8/week
- **Win rate:** Maintained at 35% (quality didn't decrease)
- **Team capacity freed:** 14 hours/week for billable work

### ROI Calculation
- **Time savings value:** 14 hours/week × $125/hour × 8 weeks = **$14,000**
- **Additional capacity for billable work:** 2 extra client projects = **$8,000**
- **Total value in 2 months:** $22,000
- **Investment:** $4,000
- **ROI:** 450% in 2 months

### Partner Quote
*"We started small to test it. After seeing how this one system changed our proposal process, we immediately upgraded to Foundation Builder to automate our reporting and lead qualification."*

## Case Study 3: Regional Property Management Company
**Industry:** Real Estate Services
**Annual Revenue:** ~$3.2M
**Employees:** 12 (management, maintenance, admin)
**Tier:** Systems Architect ($30,000 total)

### The Challenge
- Managing 180 rental units with manual processes
- Maintenance requests handled via phone/email (inconsistent tracking)
- Lease renewals required significant admin time
- Tenant screening process slow and inconsistent
- No predictive maintenance—reactive only

### Systems Built Together
1. **Intelligent Maintenance Coordinator** (Month 1-2)
   - Tenants submit requests via SMS/portal
   - AI triages urgency and routes to appropriate vendor
   - Tracks completion and collects feedback automatically

2. **Lease Renewal Agent** (Month 2-3)
   - Identifies leases due for renewal 90 days out
   - Analyzes market rates and suggests optimal pricing
   - Generates personalized renewal offers
   - Handles negotiation communications

3. **Predictive Maintenance System** (Month 3-4)
   - Analyzes maintenance history by unit age and type
   - Predicts HVAC, appliance, and system failures
   - Schedules preventative maintenance automatically
   - Orders parts proactively

4. **Tenant Screening Automation** (Month 4-5)
   - Collects application info via intelligent form
   - Runs background and credit checks automatically
   - Scores applications based on custom criteria
   - Generates approval/denial recommendations

5. **Multi-Agent Orchestration** (Month 5-6)
   - Coordinates all systems to share data
   - Creates unified dashboard for operations
   - Learns from patterns to optimize workflows

### Results (After 6 Months)
- **Admin time saved:** 35 hours/week across team
- **Maintenance response time:** From 48 hours to 6 hours average
- **Lease renewal rate:** Improved from 68% to 81%
- **Emergency maintenance reduced:** 40% (due to predictive maintenance)
- **Vacancy days reduced:** From 28 days to 19 days average
- **Tenant satisfaction:** NPS increased from 42 to 67

### ROI Calculation
- **Time savings value:** 35 hours/week × $50/hour × 24 weeks = **$42,000**
- **Reduced vacancy costs:** 9 days × 30 units/year × $1,200/month ÷ 30 days = **$10,800**
- **Higher renewal rate savings:** 13% improvement × 180 units × $800 turnover cost = **$18,720**
- **Reduced emergency maintenance:** 40% × $45,000 annual = **$18,000**
- **Total value in 6 months:** $89,520
- **Investment:** $30,000
- **ROI:** 198% in 6 months

### Owner Quote
*"We didn't just buy software—we learned how to think like AI builders. Now our team identifies automation opportunities everywhere. This is a competitive advantage that will last for years."*
